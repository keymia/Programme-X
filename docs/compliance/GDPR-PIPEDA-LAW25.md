# Compliance Readiness (GDPR + Canada)

Date: 2026-04-28

## GDPR Readiness
- Lawful basis mapping for each processing purpose.
- Consent capture for optional communications.
- Right of access / correction / deletion workflow.
- Data retention policy by entity.
- Breach notification workflow (72h target process).

## Canada (PIPEDA) Readiness
- Accountability and designated privacy owner.
- Purpose disclosure and meaningful consent.
- Limiting collection and retention.
- Safeguards and incident handling.
- Individual access request process.

## Quebec Law 25 Readiness
- Privacy impact assessment process.
- Default privacy settings for products.
- Confidentiality incident register.
- Data governance policy publication.

## Operational Checklist
1. Assign privacy officer.
2. Publish privacy policy and cookie policy.
3. Configure DSR endpoint and SLA.
4. Configure backup retention + deletion schedule.
5. Maintain incident response and annual tabletop drills.
